The Man

Omar Souhaili

Born July 19, 1989, in Casablanca, raised in the streets of Bin Lmdoun — DROS is an acronym for Da Rhymes of Streets.

Omar Souhaili didn't just rap his way to the top — he engineered his ascent with surgical precision. From battle circuits to Warner Music boardrooms, every move has been deliberate, cultural, and undeniably Moroccan.

A fierce advocate for Darija, he believes Moroccan rap has done more to elevate the dialect's "cool factor" than any other cultural medium. His 2019 diss track Moutanabbi — named after the legendary Arab poet — wasn't just a battle rap. It was a statement: this is modern high poetry.
